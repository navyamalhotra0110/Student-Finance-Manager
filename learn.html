Investment Basics
📊
Beginner Friendly

Mutual Funds

A pool of money from many investors, managed by professional fund managers. Your money is invested across stocks, bonds, or both — spreading risk automatically.

How it works: You buy "units" of a fund. As the fund's assets grow, so does your unit value (NAV).
Low Risk Options Diversified ₹500 Min
📅
Best for Students

SIP — Systematic Investment Plan

Invest a fixed amount every month automatically. Even ₹500/month builds massive wealth over time thanks to compound interest. Perfect for students with limited income.

Power of compounding: ₹1,000/month at 12% for 20 years = ₹9.9 lakhs invested → ₹39.9 lakhs returned!
Auto-debit Compounding ₹100 Min SIP
🏛️
Zero Risk

Fixed Deposit (FD)

Deposit money with a bank for a fixed period at a guaranteed interest rate. 100% safe. Ideal for parking emergency funds or short-term savings goals.

Current rates: Banks offer 6.5%–7.5% per year on FDs. Some small finance banks offer up to 9%.
Guaranteed Returns DICGC Protected Flexi FDs
📈
Higher Risk / Higher Reward

Stock Market

Buy ownership (shares) in companies. When the company grows, your investment grows. High potential returns but also higher risk — requires research and patience.

Start smart: Begin with index funds (Nifty 50, Sensex) before picking individual stocks.
High Potential Research Needed Long Term
🔒
Tax Saving

PPF — Public Provident Fund

Government-backed long-term savings (15 years). Earn ~7.1% interest, tax-free. Deposits qualify for Section 80C tax deduction. Best for building a retirement corpus early.

Key benefit: All returns are completely tax-free — even at maturity. Start early for maximum benefit.
Tax Free Govt Guaranteed
🥇
Alternative Asset

Digital Gold & SGBs

Buy gold online in small amounts (as little as ₹1) via apps like PhonePe, Zerodha. Sovereign Gold Bonds (SGBs) also give 2.5% extra interest on top of gold price appreciation.

Why gold? Acts as a hedge against inflation. Great for portfolio diversification alongside mutual funds.

⚡ Quick Wisdom for Student Investors
Start Now, Not Later 🚀
₹1,000/month from age 20 beats ₹5,000/month from age 30. Time is your unfair advantage.
Never Invest Emergency Money 🛡️
Keep 3-6 months of expenses in an FD or savings account before you invest a single rupee.
Diversify Always 🎲
Don't put all eggs in one basket. Split across FD, mutual funds, and a small equity allocation.
Avoid FOMO Investing 😤
When everyone is talking about a stock, it's usually too late. Never invest based on social media hype.
Learn Before You Earn 📚
Read "Let's Talk Money" by Monika Halan — the best personal finance book for young Indians.
